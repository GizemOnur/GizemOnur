Pinup app

During my traineeship at BeCode, I completed an internship at Pinup app, a Belgian startup that showcases local bars, restaurants, and events. At Pinup, I led the UI/UX design and Front-End Development contributions, playing a key role in ideating and implementing a specialized prototype for Horeca and Event professionals.

Using Figma, React.js, and Material UI, I contributed to shaping the project's inception. On this page, you'll find a selection of my work from this internship, providing insight into my involvement with this innovative venture.
