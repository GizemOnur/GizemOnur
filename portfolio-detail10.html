Sneax

With the Figma demo you can switch between a log in/ sign in page, a wishlist page, a shopping card page, a category menu. You can also add a like to the items you want in your wishlist or see the details and photo galery of a product you want. You can also rate the products and chose your shoe size.


Sneax is a personal project that I completed as part of my UI/UX Design training. The goal of the project was to create a fictional website for selling sneakers.

One of the main challenges was to create a website that would appeal to sneaker enthusiasts and provide a seamless user experience. To achieve this, I incorporated several animations throughout the site to make it feel dynamic and engaging.
