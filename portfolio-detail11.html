Embie Internship


During my internship at Embie, I led a UI/UX design project to overhaul the company's dashboard, known as the "kernel." Here's a brief overview of the key components:

  • Login Page: Developed an engaging and user-friendly entry point to the dashboard.
  • Timesheet Submission: Designed an intuitive interface for employees to effortlessly fill out and submit their timesheets.
  • Expense Reports: Created a streamlined process for employees to declare and manage their expense reports efficiently.
  • Visualization: Established distinct interfaces for employees and owners to visualize projects, with the owner interface featuring a dedicated financial section.
  • Profile Page: Crafted a personalized user profile page to enhance the overall user experience.

    • This comprehensive redesign not only focused on elevating the visual appeal but also prioritized functionality, ensuring a cohesive and efficient user experience across various facets of the Embie dashboard.
