Urbi Leaf Internship

During my communication studies, I interned at Urbi Leaf, specializing in Graphic/Web design and Community Management, including managing the brand's Instagram page .

This page showcases screens of the content i created for social media, web design work for the start-up's website and examples of brochures I created for the store.

URBI Leaf is an urban farm in the heart of Brussels dedicated to the cultivation of microgreens. The start-up aims to reclaim the city as a cultural space and find innovative and alternative production methods with a low ecological footprint.
